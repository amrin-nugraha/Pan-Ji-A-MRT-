UX Proposal · Room III Design Notes
The Mandala room is intentionally read-only. No inputs here — only observation. The radial SVG at center reflects the four archetypes in real-time orbit. KPI cards collapse to sparklines on narrow screens. The activity feed is the system's heartbeat — it should feel like watching the ocean, not a spreadsheet.

Additional consideration: A "Tension Heatmap by Domain × Country" could be added as a fifth panel below — a matrix grid showing where epistemic divide is most acute across the Panji zone. This would be the Mandala's most actionable output for a policy team briefing.
